About This Page

We used a library of speeches from both candidates and markov chains to generate responses to your questions. We all have topics that we want to hear the candidates discuss, and we realized that we could use arkov chains to do it! So enter a topic and watch as we generate a conversation between Clinton and Trump on your subject.

About Us

We are a team of four developers from all over the world who stumbled into each other at HackMIT 2016. The back-end was built by Alex Olson and David Robinson in Python and Flask. The front-end was built by Bianca Morris and Emmanuel Rujoni in HTML, CSS, JQuery and Bootstrap.
